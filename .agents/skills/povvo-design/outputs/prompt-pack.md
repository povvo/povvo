# Prompt Pack

## Canonical Prompt Contract

Use one identity system and one generation direction. The only brand forms are the interrupted-aperture `P` integrated as the first letter of `POVVO`, that same `P` detached as a standalone mark, and exact outline constructions of those same forms. The outline state must trace the filled state rather than reinterpret it.

## Final Positive Prompt

```text
Create a wide Povvo design-language banner on a warm off-white measured field. Make the exact word POVVO the dominant identity, rendered as a heavy forward-oblique custom wordmark. Its first letter is the canonical interrupted-aperture P: a broad circular bowl cut by one clean horizontal opening, joined to a firm vertical stem, engineered so the identical P can detach and stand alone. Show the filled black POVVO wordmark and its exact pale-cyan outline construction as matched expressions of one geometry; include one smaller detached P using that identical silhouette. Add sparse pale-cyan ruler ticks, registration crosses, vector nodes, long construction guides, tiny nonverbal measurement marks, subtle paper grain, restrained halftone texture, and controlled scan-edge falloff. Keep black and warm off-white visually dominant and cyan strictly subordinate as fine instrument linework. Preserve large areas of active negative space and precise alignment. Render POVVO correctly and render no other readable words.
```

## Locked Invariants

- The integrated and detached `P` are geometrically identical.
- Filled and outline states are exact counterparts.
- `POVVO` is the only readable text.
- Black and warm off-white dominate; pale cyan is hairline construction only.
- Registration marks, nodes, ticks, grain, and scan falloff remain sparse and subordinate.
- The composition is planar, print-like, measured, and quiet.

## Negative Constraints

```text
No alternate logo, substitute icon, unrelated symbol, modular blob, split ribbon, linked-letter emblem, extra monogram, extra readable text, people, faces, bodies, sport activity, vehicles, product photography, lifestyle imagery, transparent objects, glossy materials, saturated blue or cyan panels, neon lighting, holograms, control-room interfaces, generic futuristic technology, rounded promotional cards, heavy gradients, dramatic 3D depth, or decorative marks that compete with POVVO.
```

## Repair Command

```text
Preserve the measured off-white field, black-and-cyan hierarchy, sparse registration details, grain, and composition. Replace every brand mark with the canonical interrupted-aperture P. Use that exact P as the first letter of POVVO and as the detached standalone mark. Make every outline an exact trace of the corresponding filled geometry. Remove all other readable text, substitute symbols, product imagery, and saturated blue areas.
```

## Failure Checks And Acceptance Gates

Pass only when `POVVO` is spelled correctly, the interrupted-aperture `P` is identical in integrated and standalone use, filled and outline states match, no competing mark exists, no extra readable text appears, cyan remains subordinate, and no excluded subject or material enters the image.

## Handoff Metadata

Model, dimensions, aspect ratio, seed, reference controls, output path, and review notes remain outside model-facing prompt text. Output filenames use stable semantic names.
