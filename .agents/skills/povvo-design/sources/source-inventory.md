## Source Modes

Brand artifacts, visual narrative, product or packaging, imagery crop, and moodboard/image-set handling inform the source record. UI screenshots, physical environment, and written-brief modes are not represented.

## Exclusions

People, faces, bodies, products, source names, copied marks, sport subject matter, source slogans, and literal source layouts are excluded from production guidance. Saturated cyan is excluded as a dominant brand color. Only abstract visual signals enter the source-free design language.

## Contradictions

Handling: discard the subject and material while retaining only the filled-to-outline transition. X03 includes a person. Handling: discard the subject and retain only halftone crop and print texture.
