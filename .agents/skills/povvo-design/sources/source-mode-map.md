# Source Mode Map

## Mode Handling

| Source mode | Translate | Watch for | Output implications |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Brand artifacts | identity board, compressed type, construction lettering | copying source marks | visual DNA, type, iconography |
| Visual narrative | scan aperture, sparse-dense-sparse timing | single-frame guessing | motion, sequence, loading |
| Product or packaging | alternation between finished silhouette and line construction | importing the object or its material | filled and outline identity states |
| Imagery crop | halftone grain, mask, close crop | people as subject | imagery and texture only |
| Moodboard set | repeated field, anchor, cyan line, ticks | outlier inflation | palette, space, prompts |

## Source Handling

X01 through X06 form the provenance set. The evidence copies live in `sources/six-exemplars/`; extracted color metrics live in `sources/six-exemplars/derived/`. Production output rebuilds from source-free rules rather than reusing source images.

## Watch For

Do not average black and white into generic grey. Do not mistake pale cyan scan trace for a saturated blue brand system. Do not transfer source objects, transparent material, people, or sport subject matter into Povvo content.

## Output Implications

Design-use files stay source-free. Proof and handoff files keep evidence labels, custody, rights, and residual risk. Foundations describe paired field and board modes plus exact filled and outline identity states. Components describe measured UI translations rather than copied source layouts.
