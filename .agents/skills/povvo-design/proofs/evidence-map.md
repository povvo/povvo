## Conflicts

Dark and pale modes are kept separate. Source objects and material are discarded. People and sport content are excluded. Cyan is treated as line and glow because metrics and inspection do not support a dominant saturated color system.

## Gaps

Forms, alerts, responsive navigation, and platform behavior are inferred from the graphic language. They require accessibility review and implementation testing before release.
