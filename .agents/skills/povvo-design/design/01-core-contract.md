# Povvo Spec-Scan: Core Contract

## Use Contract

Povvo Spec-Scan is the source-free design language for Povvo. It governs identity, generated images, landing pages, UI mockups, banners, wallpapers, and reviewable design-system artifacts. A correct output is anchored by the canonical interrupted-aperture `P`: integrated as the first letter of `POVVO`, detachable as the standalone mark, and identical across filled and outline construction states. The surrounding field is measured rather than decorated: off-white or black, sparse pale cyan linework, tiny registration marks, visible construction, restrained texture, and controlled density.

Non-negotiables:

- The language name is exactly Povvo Spec-Scan.
- The interrupted-aperture `P` is the only primary mark. Its geometry is identical inside `POVVO` and when used alone.
- The canonical identity set is the filled `POVVO`, outline-construction `POVVO`, filled standalone `P`, and outline standalone `P`.
- Black and off-white dominate; cyan is an instrument for line, focus, scan, or rare accent.
- Display type is compressed, slanted or compact, and object-like; task text is plain and readable.
- People and sport subject matter are not Povvo content.
- Unrelated icons, modular blobs, alternate logo geometries, and product-object stand-ins are forbidden.
- Build from named tokens, component recipes, and pattern rules before inventing new choices.
- Never rely on colour, motion, texture, position, or tiny text as the only carrier of required information.

## Design DNA

Core territory: the integrated `POVVO` wordmark, standalone interrupted-aperture `P`, matched filled and outline construction states, off-white spec sheet, black identity board, optical scan aperture, and halftone print texture. These territories share a strict grammar: one canonical identity anchor, a measured field, small instrument marks, and disciplined silence.

Emotional register: quiet inspection pressure. The language feels precise, watchful, and graphic rather than fast or luxurious. Intensity is controlled through contrast and crop. Delight is a small alignment discovery, a visible vector handle, or a sudden but bounded density burst.

Signature grammar: a heavy oblique `POVVO` wordmark whose first `P` has one horizontal aperture through a broad rounded-rectangular bowl; the same `P` detached as the icon; exact filled and outline duplicates; off-white paper/screen fields; near-black identity anchors; pale cyan hairlines; ruler ticks; registration crosses; short slash bars; diagonal construction guides; circular scan falloff; low paper grain; and compact calibration marks.

Core principles: one mark, one geometry; measure before decorating; black carries weight; cyan reveals construction; density arrives in bursts; construction remains visible; identity outranks decoration.

Primary anti-patterns: alternate P shapes, unrelated standalone icons, modular pieces, saturated cyan brand fields, generic dark control panels, glossy interface glass, product or lifestyle imagery, people as subject, sport cues, generic cards, decorative blobs, and illegible generated microcopy.

Outlier handling: human or product source subjects never enter production imagery. Their only translated consequences are warm print grain, tight crop behavior, and the principle that a finished filled identity may be paired with its exact outline construction state.

Establishment and subversion: establish the canonical P/POVVO identity, palette, space, surface, and interaction grammar before density or motion. Use at least three stable cues before one density burst, then return to the measured field.

## Reconstructive Fidelity

Canonical frame types: identity banner, measured white field, black identity board, outline construction sheet, scan aperture, and UI spec panel.

Geometry, crop, and lighting: Use sharp rectangular fields, diagonal guides, hard crop masks, and ruler edges. Allow large display type to crop at viewport edges only when `POVVO` remains identifiable and the interrupted-aperture P remains intact. Keep body copy inside safe zones. Lighting is frontal and print-like on fields with controlled edge falloff. Shadows are shallow; borders and contrast do most separation.

Source-free reconstruction recipe:

1. Choose the canonical frame: identity banner, white field, black board, construction sheet, scan aperture, or UI panel.
2. Set the palette: off-white or near-black base, black or pale inverse anchor, muted cyan linework, optional low grain.
3. Place one identity anchor: integrated `POVVO` or the standalone interrupted-aperture `P`; do not substitute another symbol.
4. Add measured marks: ruler ticks, registration crosses, vector handles, slash bars, guide lines, and micro labels.
5. Add the matched outline state when construction, identity proof, focus, reveal, loading, or sequence requires it.
6. Check drift boundaries before output.

Replication checks: palette fidelity passes when black/off-white dominate; type fidelity passes when display anchors compress and body copy stays readable; spatial fidelity passes when the composition has field, axis, and silence; surface fidelity passes when UI stays flat; interaction fidelity passes when states have labels, structure, and reduced-motion equivalents.
