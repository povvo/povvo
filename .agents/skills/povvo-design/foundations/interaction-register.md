# Interaction Register

## Interaction Register

## Ambient Atmosphere

Held inspection: a field waiting to be measured.

## Percussive Events

Focus is a bracket, selection is a black fill or double frame, loading is scan or tick rail, error is a repair tick, success is a stable check.

## Feedback Density

One structural mark, one text state, and at most one motion event per action.

## Silence

Silence can mean readiness, but not for errors, focus, or loading delays.

## Trust Construction

Stable axes, repeatable state behavior, direct recovery, and readable labels.

## State Semantics

| State | Visual change | Copy behavior | Accessibility rule |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Default | off-white field, black text, cyan only as structural hairline | short noun or verb | normal text 4.5:1 |
| Hover | hairline thickens or anchor darkens | no required new copy | not color-only |
| Focus | 2px cyan bracket plus black or pale tick | accessible name present | 3:1 non-text contrast |
| Active | ink compresses or surface settles | label stays visible | no target movement |
| Selected | black fill, double frame, or underline | selected named when ambiguous | shape backup |
| Disabled | muted ink and removed emphasis | reason when useful | readable explanation |
| Loading | scan line, tick rail, or skeleton field | short progress phrase | reduced branch |
| Error | red-orange tick, black label, repair text | direct repair instruction | not color-only |
| Warning | dark or amber tick interruption | consequence before action | icon or shape backup |
| Success | blue-green check or stable tick | past-tense confirmation | motion optional |

Povvo Spec-Scan is a measured graphic language centered on one canonical identity system: the interrupted-aperture P integrated into the POVVO wordmark and detached as the standalone mark. Warm off-white and ink black carry the field; pale cyan exposes outline construction, ruler ticks, registration marks, vector nodes, and scan edges. The wordmark and P use matched filled and outline states. No unrelated symbol or modular shape may replace the P. Active silence, low print grain, and controlled optical falloff complete the language. People, sport subject matter, product imagery, and generic futuristic interfaces are excluded.

Operational terms covered here: Ambient Atmosphere, Feedback Density, State Semantics. These terms are used as build rules, not placeholders. Every artifact should be reconstructable without reopening proof material: choose a measured field or black board, place the integrated POVVO wordmark or standalone interrupted-aperture P as the identity anchor, expose its matched outline construction only where useful, and add only measured marks needed for alignment or state.

## Rules

- Black and off-white carry the system's visible weight.
- Muted cyan identifies instrument lines, focus brackets, scan falloff, and rare accents.
- Dense information appears as a bounded burst, then returns to a quiet field.
- Display type can crop and compress; body and state text stay readable.
- Source subjects are not reused; the language is standalone.

## Drift Controls

Fidelity check: preserve the interrupted-aperture P, identical integrated and standalone geometry, matched filled and outline POVVO states, black and off-white dominance, pale cyan hairlines, tiny measured marks, active negative space, scan or bracket state logic, and exact text control. Reject alternate logo geometry, unrelated symbols, modular blobs, saturated cyan panels, glossy control-room styling, generic cards, product or lifestyle imagery, people-led subjects, sport content, copied source marks, and extra generated words.
