# Typographic Voice

## Typographic Voice

## Voice Weight

Display is heavy, compressed, and object-like; body is plain and readable; micro labels are measured.

## Voice Classification

| Type category | Role | Why it fits |
| --- | --- | --- |
| condensed display | anchor | forms graphic slabs |
| neutral grotesk | body | stays readable |
| square grotesk or mono | micro labels | supports measurement |
| outline construction | diagram state | shows built form |

## Type Pairing Logic

Use posture and scale contrast, not decorative family mixing.

## Text As Spatial Element

Type can be object, annotation, ruler label, or compact placard.

## Dialogue Density

Low by default, dense only in bounded clusters.

## Silence As Whitespace

Absent text means measured readiness.

## Scale Relationship

| Role | Family | Weight | Size range | Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Display anchor | condensed italic or extended grotesk | 800 to 900 | 48 to 180px | object wordmarks and cropped anchors |
| Section heading | condensed grotesk | 700 to 800 | 24 to 48px | short panel titles |
| Body | neutral grotesk | 450 to 650 | 15 to 18px | readable task copy |
| Micro label | square grotesk or mono fallback | 600 to 700 | 10 to 12px | ticks, metadata, coordinates |
| Utility numeral | tabular mono or grotesk | 600 to 800 | 11 to 18px | indexes and values |

Povvo Spec-Scan is a measured graphic language centered on one canonical identity system: the interrupted-aperture P integrated into the POVVO wordmark and detached as the standalone mark. Warm off-white and ink black carry the field; pale cyan exposes outline construction, ruler ticks, registration marks, vector nodes, and scan edges. The wordmark and P use matched filled and outline states. No unrelated symbol or modular shape may replace the P. Active silence, low print grain, and controlled optical falloff complete the language. People, sport subject matter, product imagery, and generic futuristic interfaces are excluded.

Operational terms covered here: Voice Weight, Voice Classification, Scale Relationship. These terms are used as build rules, not placeholders. Every artifact should be reconstructable without reopening proof material: choose a measured field or black board, place the integrated POVVO wordmark or standalone interrupted-aperture P as the identity anchor, expose its matched outline construction only where useful, and add only measured marks needed for alignment or state.

## Rules

- Black and off-white carry the system's visible weight.
- Muted cyan identifies instrument lines, focus brackets, scan falloff, and rare accents.
- Dense information appears as a bounded burst, then returns to a quiet field.
- Display type can crop and compress; body and state text stay readable.
- Source subjects are not reused; the language is standalone.

## Drift Controls

Fidelity check: preserve the interrupted-aperture P, identical integrated and standalone geometry, matched filled and outline POVVO states, black and off-white dominance, pale cyan hairlines, tiny measured marks, active negative space, scan or bracket state logic, and exact text control. Reject alternate logo geometry, unrelated symbols, modular blobs, saturated cyan panels, glossy control-room styling, generic cards, product or lifestyle imagery, people-led subjects, sport content, copied source marks, and extra generated words.
